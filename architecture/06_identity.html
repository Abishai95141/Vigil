06 · v3 §9 Two-tier identity

The pod restarts. The identity doesn't.

Every restart in Kubernetes creates a new UID. Without a logical identity, history dies on every restart. Vigil tracks both — the ephemeral PodInstance and the stable PodRole — so the operator can ask coherent questions across time.
